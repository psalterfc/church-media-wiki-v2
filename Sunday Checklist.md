# Sunday Quick Checklist

## Before Service
- [ ] Power on all devices
- [ ] Cameras working
- [ ] Audio levels moving
- [ ] OBS showing video
- [ ] Internet working

## During Service
- [ ] Start stream
- [ ] Switch cameras correctly
- [ ] Show lyrics when needed
- [ ] Monitor audio

## After Service
- [ ] Stop stream
- [ ] Turn off overlays
- [ ] Shutdown systems
